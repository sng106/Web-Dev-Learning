Biriyani Recipie

Biryani is a celebrated mixed rice dish that serves as a centerpiece of South Asian cuisine. It features long-grain Basmati rice layered with marinated meat (usually chicken, mutton, or beef) or vegetables, flavored with a complex blend of spices including cardamom, cinnamon, cloves, and saffron.

Instructions

  • Basmati Rice
  • Meat (Chicken or Mutton)
  • Biryani Spices (Cardamom, Cloves, Cinnamon)
  • Saffron or Food Coloring

Steps

  1. Mix the meat with ginger-garlic paste, yogurt, chili powder, and biryani spices. Let it sit for at least 30 minutes to absorb the flavors.
  2. Boil the Basmati rice in salted water with whole spices (cloves, cardamom). Cook it until it is only 70% done (it should still have a slight bite), then drain it.
  3. In a heavy-bottomed pot, place the marinated meat at the bottom and spread the partially cooked rice over it. Add fried onions, fresh mint, and saffron-infused milk on top.
  4. Seal the pot with a tight lid (or dough) to trap the steam. Cook on very low heat for 20–30 minutes, allowing the rice to finish cooking in the meat's juices.
